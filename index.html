MEET THE DOCTOR

Dr.Paul polyviou

Dr. Paul Polyviou has been practicing dentistry since 1995. Raised in the downriver area, Dr. Polyviou received his undergraduate degree from Wayne State University and earned his dental degree from the University of Detroit Mercy School of Dentistry. Dr. Polyviou is truly committed to providing the highest quality dental care to his patients. As such, he spends countless hours outside of the office pursuing continuing education courses so that he can maintain the best dentistry for all his patients.
